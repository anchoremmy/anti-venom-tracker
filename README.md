🇿🇦 National Anti-Venom Distribution & Risk Tracker (MVP)

Executive Summary
This platform is a Strategic Health Intelligence Dashboard designed to solve the critical visibility gap in South Africa's snakebite emergency response. By integrating real-time hospital inventory with geospatial bio-hazard risk maps, we empower the National Department of Health (NDoH) to save lives through data-driven resource allocation.

🚀 Key Capabilities
Real-Time Inventory Mapping: Interactive visualization of anti-venom stock levels across South Africa’s provincial hospitals.
Predictive Heatmaps: Overlays historical snakebite "hotspots" (based on ASI data) against current supply to identify "Crisis Zones."
Decentralized Admin Portal: A secure interface for hospital pharmacists to update local stock levels instantly without technical training.
Emergency Action Center: One-click "Emergency Transfer Requests" to alert Provincial Health Departments of stock-outs in high-risk areas.

🛠️ Technology Stack
Frontend/Logic: Streamlit (Python-based Rapid Prototyping)
Geospatial Visualization: PyDeck & Mapbox
Data Management: Pandas (Transitioning to Supabase/PostgreSQL for Production)
Deployment: Streamlit Community Cloud (CI/CD via GitHub)

🛡️ Compliance & Safety
POPIA: Designed with privacy-first architecture.
NDoH Standards: Aligning with the National Digital Health Strategy (2025-2029).
